CLIENT LOGOS FOLDER
===================

Place your client logo files here (SVG or PNG format recommended).

Logo Guidelines:
- Format: SVG (preferred) or PNG with transparent background
- Height: ~28-32px for consistency
- Color: White or light gray logos work best on black background
- Naming: Use lowercase with hyphens (e.g., company-name.svg)

To customize:
- Replace these files with your actual client logos
- Or update the src paths in index.html to match your logo filenames
- Keep similar heights for visual consistency

CSS adjustments (in style.css):
- Change opacity (currently 0.5) to make logos brighter/darker
- Adjust animation speed (currently 28s) - lower = faster
- Modify gap between logos (currently 56px, 40px on mobile)
